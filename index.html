I'm a college student studying Computer Science and Film! This site is created and maintained by me and all the photos you see are taken by me!

This project was mainly created as a way to start building a portfolio of pictures! I finally got the chance to obtain a professional camera this past year and I wanted a reason to go out and take pictures with it. I mainly focus on areas and things I find interesting while also trying to capture a certain feel with all the pictures I take, and I hope you can feel that same feeling.
