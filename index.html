Hey! Welcome to my site!

I am a college student currently studying Computer Science and Film! I like think that you can have your cake AND eat it too!

I currently use a Canon EOS Rebel T3i to capture and Adobe Lightroom to develop.

With the knowledge I currently have, I set this website up myself!

I currently utilize GitHub's hosting service they offer for free, but plans are made to switch to a more stable service eventually.

Hope you enjoy! :)
