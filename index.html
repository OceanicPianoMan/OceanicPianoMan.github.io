Pereyra Perspective

by Julian Pereyra

Hello! I'm a college student studying Computer Science and Film! This site was created and is maintained by me and all the photos you see are also taken by me.

This project was mainly created as a way to start building a portfolio of pictures. I finally got the chance to obtain a professional camera this past year and I wanted a real reason to go out and take pictures with it. I mainly focus on areas or things I find interesting while also trying to capture a certain feel with all the pictures I take, and I hope you can find that same feeling.
